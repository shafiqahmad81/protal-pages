We are Portal

Portal is the first video streaming platform to run 100% on-chain. The power of the blockchain opens up exciting new possibilities such as fair and transparent revenue models, automated royalty distributions, fractionalized ownership of channels, community governance and more.

Streaming video on-chain is currently only possible on the Internet Computer blockchain. Why Portal Is The Future of Video

Portal technology provides a compelling case for a truly decentralized Web3 video solution

1

Operating Cost

Portal is able to operate at a fraction of the cost of traditional streaming platforms, thanks to the Internet Computer. Portal can then share the vast majority of the revenue generated per view with the video creator.Lower operating costs mean higher revenue splits and incentives for creators.

2

Blockchain Technology

Portal consists of smart contracts running entirely on the the blockchain. These contracts provide the required functionality for delivering high-quality video streaming experiences comparable to traditional platforms.

The smart contracts include systems for transactions, invoicing, ownership models, governance, media storage, DRM, user identity, ratings, reviews, tax records, and incentivized referral links.

3

True Ownership

On traditional platforms videos and content are stored on company owned servers. Platforms can ban users without warning, and content, comments, likes/dislikes, shares, followers, and metadata are all lost.

With Portal, the creator will always remain the owner of their channel. And thanks to blockchain technology, the creator never has to put their content in the hands of a centralized company.
